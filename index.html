Who We Are

iLemoja Ventures Limited is a pioneering Kenyan-based social enterprise integrating innovation consulting, venture capital, and sustainable development. We are dedicated to fostering economic growth, environmental stewardship, and social equity across Africa. Our holistic approach leverages cutting-edge strategies and strategic investments to empower local communities and drive impactful, scalable solutions.

Portfolio

Notable Projects & Initiatives

Each initiative is designed as part of a broader ecosystem, enabling shared infrastructure, scalable deployment, and long-term impact.

Smart Cities
Bin Bag Solutions

Smart Waste Management & Circular Economy Platform

Advanced urban waste management system with solar-powered, self-compacting smart bins integrated with a digital monitoring and collection platform. Improves waste efficiency and supports cleaner, sustainable cities.

♻️
Urban Mobility
Supasako

Public Transport Investment & Urban Mobility Platform

A structured investment and governance platform connecting individual investors, transport SACCOs, and public-sector stakeholders, enabling pooled capital formation and data-driven transport management.

🚌
Infrastructure
Safe Zone

Inclusive Urban Safety & Smart Infrastructure

An urban design and infrastructure initiative focused on creating secure, accessible, and inclusive public spaces. By integrating smart lighting, surveillance, connectivity, and human-centered design, Safe Zone enhances public safety.

🏙️
Construction
iCube

Modular Living, Work & Commercial Space Solutions

A modular construction venture delivering prefabricated cubes for residential, retail, and office use. Designed for rapid deployment, flexibility, and affordability to address urban space constraints and rising construction costs.

🏗️
Research
iLab

Research, Design & Systems Innovation Laboratory

iLemoja's strategic research and experimentation platform focused on design-driven innovation and systems thinking. Supports concept development, piloting, and validation across smart cities, clean technology, and mobility.

🔬
FinTech
iLemoja Cash

Integrated Digital Payments & Ecosystem Finance Layer

A unified digital payments and financial services platform designed to interconnect iLemoja's ventures and partners, enabling seamless transactions, loyalty programs, and future integration with transport systems and public services.

💳
Portfolio Logic: Collectively, these initiatives form a cohesive, platform-based ecosystem — shared infrastructure, shared data, and shared value creation — positioning iLemoja Ventures as a long-term urban innovation and systems builder rather than a collection of standalone projects.
